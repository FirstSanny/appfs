627  relative and/or absolute precision.
628  assert_array_almost_equal_nulp, assert_array_max_ulp, assert_equal

633  >>> np.testing.assert_approx_equal(0.12345670e-20, 0.12345671e-20,
634  significant=8)

1932  attributes mirror the arguments to ``showwarning()``.
